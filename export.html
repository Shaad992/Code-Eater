1. Company Registration & Related Expenses

Dubai me export business start karne ke liye sabse pehla step hota hai company registration. Aapko Dubai’s Department of Economic Development (DED) ya kisi Free Zone authority ke under apni company register karani hoti hai. Steps include: Trade name reservation Initial approval MOA (Memorandum of Association) drafting Office lease agreement (Ejari) Trade license issuance Expenses may include: License fees (AED 10,000–15,000 depending on activity) Office space rent (Free zone or mainland specific) Visa processing fees Immigration card & establishment card fees

2. Required Certificates for Export

Dubai se legal export karne ke liye kuch mandatory certificates ki zarurat hoti hai: Certificate of Origin (Chamber of Commerce se) Commercial Invoice Packing List Export Declaration (via Dubai Customs portal) Phytosanitary Certificate (for wood or plant-based goods) Bill of Lading / Airway Bill (depending on shipment mode) Insurance Certificate (optional but recommended)

3. Working with LC (Letter of Credit) via Bank

LC ek secure financial instrument hai jo exporter aur importer ke beech trust build karta hai. Process: Buyer (importer) apne bank se LC open karta hai LC ke terms predefined hote hain (amount, timeline, documents required, port details) Seller (exporter) apne bank ko LC receive hone par shipment prepare karta hai.

4. How Payment Happens to Seller under LC

Jab seller LC ke terms ke mutabiq saare documents prepare karta hai, to ye steps follow hote hain: i. Seller bank ko required documents deta hai (Invoice, B/L, Certificate of Origin etc.) ii. Bank documents ko verify karta hai iii. Agar sab kuch LC terms ke mutabiq hai, to bank payment release karta hai seller ke account me iv. Uske baad bank documents buyer ke bank ko forward karta hai Note: Ye pura process buyer aur seller dono ke liye risk-free banata hai.

5. Important Factors to Consider in Export

Export karte waqt kuch key points hamesha dhyaan me rakhne chahiye: Product compliance with destination country regulations Correct HS code and customs duty structure Accurate documentation to avoid clearance delays Proper packaging and labeling Incoterms clarity (FOB, CIF, EXW etc.) Freight insurance Timely communication with freight forwarders & logistics partners.

6. Important Documents to Collect from Buyer

Exporter ko buyer se kuch crucial documents aur confirmations collect karne chahiye: Signed Purchase Order or Sales Contract Confirmed LC draft (agar LC mode hai) Import License copy (if applicable in buyer’s country) Delivery Instructions Port of Discharge details Any regulatory approvals needed for that country

7. Freezone – A Strategic Export Advantage

Dubai ke Freezones jaise JAFZA, DMCC, DAFZA, Dubai South exporters ke liye bohot beneficial hain. Benefits: 100% foreign ownership allowed No import/export duty within Freezone No personal or corporate income tax (up to 50 years) Easy company setup with minimal bureaucracy Strategic warehouse and port access (e.g., Jebel Ali Port) Faster customs clearance & dedicated logistics services Note: Freezone se export karte waqt agar goods UAE mainland me nahi ja rahe to aapko VAT nahi dena hota.
